## How to Invest: Step-by-Step Guide
--------------------------------------

### Step 1: Open Demat Account

*   Visit the website of your preferred brokerage firm (e.g., Zerodha, Upstox, etc.) and apply for a demat account.
*   Fill in the required details and submit the application form.
*   Complete the KYC (Know Your Customer) process by uploading documents as per the broker's requirements.

### Step 2: Fund the Demat Account

*   Deposit funds into your demat account using various payment modes such as NEFT, RTGS, UPI, etc.
*   Verify that the funds have been credited to your account.

### Step 3: Invest in Equities

1.  **HDFC Bank**: Search for HDFC Bank shares on your broker's platform and click on 'Buy'.
2.  **Infosys**: Follow the same process for Infosys shares.
3.  **Tata Consultancy Services**: Repeat the process for Tata Consultancy Services shares.

### Step 4: Invest in Fixed Income

1.  **Government Securities (G Secs)**: Log in to your demat account and navigate to the fixed income section.
2.  **Corporate Bonds**: Select the desired corporate bond from the available options and click on 'Buy'.

### Step 5: Invest in Commodities

1.  **Gold ETF**: Search for Gold ETF shares on your broker's platform and click on 'Buy'.
2.  **Bitcoin/Ethereum**: Follow the same process, taking into account the specific requirements for cryptocurrency investments.
